Welcome to Pet Adoption

Pet Adoption is a non-profit organization located in Bengaluru, Karnataka-India, where we provide a service to the homeless dogs and cats population, exposed to abuses, hunger and often, a cruel death..
We transform rescued dogs and cats (sometimes very scared or shy) into safe pets for the right people-companions.
